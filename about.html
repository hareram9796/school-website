Our story, vision, and commitment to excellence

Our Story

25 Years of Shaping Bright Futures

Sri Vidya Academy was founded in 1999 by Dr. K. Venkatesh Rao with a vision to provide world-class education to every child, regardless of background. What started as a small school with 200 students has grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in Hyderabad.

Today, we are home to 2500+ students, 120+ dedicated faculty members, and a sprawling 5-acre campus equipped with modern facilities. Our alumni are making their mark in IITs, IIMs, medical colleges, and top universities worldwide.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution that nurtures intellectually curious, morally upright, and socially responsible citizens who contribute positively to society and the nation.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that combines academic excellence with character development, critical thinking, creativity, and compassion — preparing students for the challenges of the 21st century.

Our Values

Integrity, Excellence, Respect, Innovation, and Service. These core values guide everything we do — from classroom teaching to extracurricular activities and community engagement.
